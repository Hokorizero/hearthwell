Are you looking for the best Early Game Inventory Management Tool? Look no further, for Vaultopic has arrived!
The Vaultopic Mod includes a couple of easily crafted items that help you manage your inventories.

The V.I.E.W. (Vaultopic Inventory Explorer Workbot) lets you search for items inside nearby inventories and will display them as you move around, even through walls.








The V.I.C.E. (Vaultopic Item Crafting Entity) will let you pull items from nearby inventories into a portable crafting table, with JEI integration.
