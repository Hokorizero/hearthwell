aTV - animated Texture Viewer for Minecraft

Download aTV

A micro java app to view Minecraft item and block animations when editing the image.
Will automatically when you save. Does not read mcmeta files.
To load a new image press load. Manually reloading is also possible by pressing the update button
Double click the image to toggle between showing GUI and not showing GUI
Right click the image to choose the desired image size;
