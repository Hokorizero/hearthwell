Mechanics - Crafting Ways

Mod created in colaboration with al132mc
Download this at curseforge.

This mod adds some crafting mechanics to spice up modpacks.
By itself, this mod adds almost nothing to the game, and must be configured to add recipes.
All of the recipes are available through CraftTweaker.

Drying.

Similar to Drying Racks from Tinker's and Primal Core. Time will transform an item stack into another. Example usage: Example usage: mods.mechanics.addDryingTableRecipe(, );

Hammering.

Martlets to crack drown blocks. Comes with 3 recipes (Stone to Cobblestone, Cobblestone to Gravel and Gravel to Sand) and two varieties, stone and iron. Example usage: ods.mechanics.addMartletRecipe(, );

Alloying.

The Alloy furnace requires a fuel to transform two item stacks into one. Example usage: mods.mechanics.addAlloyFurnaceRecipe(, , );
